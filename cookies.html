Cookies Policy

This Cookies Policy explains what cookies are and how we use them. You should read this policy so you can understand what type of cookies we use, the information we collect using cookies and how that information is used. If you don’t want to accept cookies, you can change your browser settings so that cookies are not accepted. If you do this, please be aware that some features of our website may not work as intended.

What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your device by websites that you visit. They are widely used to allow a website to function (for example to keep track of your shopping basket) or to provide information to the website owner. There are different types of cookies, including:

  • Session cookies, which are deleted from your device when you close your browser
  • Persistent cookies, which remain on your device for a set period of time
  • First-party cookies, which are set by the website you are visiting
  • Third-party cookies, which are set by a third-party website separate from the website you are visiting

What cookies do we use?

We use the following types of cookies on our website:

  • Session cookies, which are used to keep track of your movements from page to page so you don’t have to authenticate or re-enter information on every page you visit.
  • Persistent cookies, which are used to remember your preferences and choices when using our website.
  • Third-party analytics cookies, which are used to gather data about how you use our website so that we can improve the user experience. These cookies are set by a third-party service provider and are not used to identify you personally.

How do we use cookies?

We use cookies for several reasons:

  • To provide a more personalized user experience
  • To remember your preferences and choices when using our website
  • To gather data about how you use our website so that we can improve the user experience

How can I control cookies?

You can control and manage cookies in several ways. Please keep in mind that removing or blocking cookies can negatively impact your user experience and parts of our website may no longer be fully accessible.

  • You can set your browser to not accept cookies, or to notify you when you receive a new cookie, giving you the chance to decide whether to accept it or not.
  • You can use your browser settings to delete all or some cookies, or to block all or some cookies.
  • You can find more information about how to control cookies using the following links:
    • Google Chrome
    • Mozilla Firefox
    • Microsoft Edge
    • Apple Safari

If you have any questions or concerns about our use of cookies, please contact us using the information provided in our Privacy Policy.
